District One Residences
in Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum City

District One Residences by Meydan Sobha Group is a range of contemporary apartments located in the opulent community of Mohammed Bin Rashid (MBR) City District.

These waterfront residences provide the perfect lagoon view over a cup of coffee every waking day. You can also take a quick stroll or jog by the boardwalk, or even go for a swim.

The apartments in District One Residences are available in 1, 2 and 3-bedroom units in low and mid-rise towers varying from four to sixteen storeys. The community is complete with everything you need from schools, retail shops, restaurants and entertainment destinations. Prices for its 1-bedroom unit start at AED 1.23 million.
